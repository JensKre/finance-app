# 1. Introduction and Goals

## 1.1 Purpose
FinanceFlow is a private financial tracking application designed for Jens and Annika to manage their shared and individual assets. It provides a real-time overview of their net worth, distribution across categories, and a 25-year growth prognosis.

## 1.2 Quality Goals
1. **Maintainability**: The codebase should be simple and easy to update (React + FastAPI).
2. **Data Integrity**: Financial records must be stored accurately and handled consistently across different views.
3. **Usability**: The interface must be modern, fast, and optimized for two primary users (Jens & Annika).

## 1.3 Stakeholders
| Role | Goal |
|---|---|
| Jens & Annika | Primary users. Want a clear view of their financial path to 2M €. |
| Developer | Needs to maintain and extend the app easily. |
